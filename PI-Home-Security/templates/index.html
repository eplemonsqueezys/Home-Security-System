Alarm System Behavior

  • When an armed zone is tripped, the alarm sound will play continuously
  • The alarm will only stop when you either disarm the entire system or specifically disarm the triggered zone
  • In "Away Mode", all zones are monitored and will trigger the alarm if tripped
  • In "Home Mode", only perimeter zones are monitored
  • Use the mute button to temporarily silence the alarm without disarming
